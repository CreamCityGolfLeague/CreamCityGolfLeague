Rules & Structure

League Format

  • Duration: April through September 2026
  • Events: 10 scoring rounds, 1 Scramble, 1 Par 3 Challenge, and a Championship
  • Flexibility: Each round lasts 2 weeks
  • Championship: Final event awards double points

Flights & Handicaps

  • Flight A: 0.0 – 6.9
  • Flight B: 7.0 – 12.9
  • Flight C: 13.0 – 18.9
  • Flight D: 19.0+
  • Handicaps: GHIN or calculated after 3 rounds

Scoring & Points

  • 1,000 total points awarded per flight each event
  • Double points at the Championship
  • Participation points for attending every event

Payouts

  • 1st Place: Low Net
  • 2nd Place: Low Net
  • 3rd Place: Low Gross
  • Ties: USGA recommendations
